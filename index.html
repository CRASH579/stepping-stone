About Stepping Stone Foundation Trust

Stepping Stone Foundation Trust is a Public Charitable Trust registered at Meerut, formed in 2015 with a noble mission to serve the underprivileged section of society.

We provide education and skill training to unemployed youth, helping them enter the mainstream workforce, become self-sufficient, and contribute to society's upliftment. Our programs operate under government schemes such as PMKVY, DDUGKY, UPSDM, and others.

We believe that skill development is the key to building a prosperous and inclusive India. With a special focus on women empowerment, rural outreach, and industry alignment, our mission goes beyond just placements‚Äîit's about building better lives.

Our Training Programs

PMKVY

Skill development with certification and placement support under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana.

NULM

A project in association with DUDA with an initiative to impart skill trainings to the needy urban youth to develop & enhance their skills for a successful future with career growth with certification.

DDU-GKY

Placement-linked training for rural youth under Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana.

UPSDM

Focused on local trades, empowering all sections of society with vocational training in Uttar Pradesh.

Project Praveen

Developing employable skills among high school students in government institutions.

Soft Skills

Essential skills like communication, computer literacy, and workplace etiquette to build confident professionals.

Women Empowerment

Dedicated projects to promote entrepreneurship, self-reliance, and home-based economic activities for rural women.

BOSCH - CSR

A CSR Project in association with BOSCH to provide training in Communication & Computer Skills to receive additional certificates which will help them in their career.

Activities Beyond Training

Beyond classroom training, Stepping Stone Foundation engages the youth in meaningful and motivating experiences:

  • Kaushal Melas: Events to raise awareness about government schemes, with guest participation from local authorities.
  • Rozgar Melas: Job fairs that connect trained candidates to companies for campus and off-campus placements.
  • Yoga Day Celebrations: Promoting wellness and mental health through regular Yoga practices.
  • World Environment Day: Tree plantations, art contests, and awareness rallies conducted by students.
  • Industry Visits: Exposure to manufacturing and corporate environments for practical insight.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To enable inclusive growth by upskilling individuals across India, making them active contributors in their communities and the nation.

Mission

To bridge the employability gap by offering holistic, certified training aligned with real industry needs and promoting entrepreneurial thinking.
